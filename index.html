Piping Guideline

Introduction of PVC Pipes

  • PVC Garden Hose Pipes are designed to facilitate transferring water from taps, tanks to gardens.
  • These pipes have great flexible, leak-proof construction, excellent pressure resistance, and longer performance life, as it has been manufactured using best in class polyvinyl chloride (PVC) complying quality standards of plastic industry.
  • These pipes are widely used in homes, bungalows, hotels, hospitals, construction sites, industrial, agricultural & plumbing industry.
  • PVC Garden Hose Pipes come with varied length, thickness, and color to select from.

Garden Pipe Applications

  1. 1. Water supply in Industrial, Domestic and Irrigation purpose
  2. 2. Gardening & Cleaning requirments
  3. 3. Water supply & leveling tube at Construction sites
  4. 4. Car/Vehicle washing
